About Unisoft

We are a leading provider of integrated business solutions. Our solutions leverage industry expertise and leading-edge technologies to solve business problems, globally, every single day.

We address more than a dozen industry verticals and focus on business process engineering and automation. We work with best-of-breed and state-of-the art technology providers such as SAP, Anaplan, IBM and AWS to enhance your enterprise competitiveness.

Our global delivery model comprises on-site consulting and delivery, off-site and off-shore delivery bridging time and geographical gaps, ensuring our client, gets the best value for money and time.

Unisoft has substantial team of consultants in the Asia Pacific region to provide the following services to our customers:

  • Implementation Services: End-to-End, Module-by-Module, and New Dimension solutions using PROMAN methodology.
  • Upgrade Services: Optimized Upgrade Services:
  • Integration Services: EAI, B2Bi, ALE and batch interfaces;
  • Consulting Services: Manufacturing, Distribution and Retail;
  • Maintenance Services: BASIS administration, Archiving, ABAP development, enhancements and system health checks;
  • Support Services: Using UNICARE methodology.
  • SAP All-in-one for solutions for various Industry Sectors. Fixed Price, Fixed Time implementation;
  • SAP B2Bi using Process Integration;
  • SAP LES Turn-key implementation;
  • SAP PLM, Data and Document Archiving using iXOS, Mobius and Easy Archive;
  • SAP BW and BOBJ Implementation;
  • Interfaces with Shop Floor/MES and MII Systems;
  • Integration with 3rd Party Products;
  • Data Migration and Conversion Strategy and Implementation

Consultants of Unisoft Infotech are highly trained and have been involved with over 175 projects in the Asia Pacific region with SAP implementation and related activities.

Quality Management System

We are driven by a passion to constantly improve the quality of our solutions and services to provide a higher level of value to our customers every time.

We aim to achieve our objectives by following our 4-point quality principle.

  • Perfect Solutions: Provide superior solution through continuous acquisition of knowhow and improvement of our service levels.
  • Better People: Ensure that talented individuals with superior aptitude and attitude are available and equipped with the knowledge of the technologies and the methodologies to deliver solutions meeting customer expectations.
  • Better Processes: Continuous innovation to improve the processes in order to enhance the service levels, thereby reducing the cost of ownership for the customer.
  • Continuous Improvement: Quality system goes through regular measurement-analysis-improvement cycle to ensure the effectiveness of QMS - quality policy and processes.
